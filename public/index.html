Message: Write in plain language, between 10 and 500 characters. One or two sentences about how you feel or what is on your mind is ideal — concrete beats vague filler.

Mood face — required: Pick exactly one tile before you submit. The relay stores and sends the face’s canonical id (e.g. star-struck, sob) — not the Unicode glyph — so OpenAI always grounds on the same label plus your text.
