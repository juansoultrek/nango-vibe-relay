Nango integration debugger.

What happens: Submit triggers validation, then OpenAI reads your message plus emoji and returns a cleaned line and interpreted mood. With env vars set, Nango proxies Google Sheets (append row) and Slack (channel post). The step log records each phase so OAuth or API failures show up next to the step that broke.

Message: Write in plain language, between 10 and 500 characters. One or two sentences about how you feel or what is on your mind is ideal — concrete beats vague filler.

Mood emoji — required: Pick exactly one face before you submit (no emoji means the relay stops at validation). The emoji gives the model an explicit tone anchor so interpreted mood and downstream summaries track how you signaled you felt, not only the words.
